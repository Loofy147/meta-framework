**Improvement Backlog Template:**
- **Item:** IMP-001
  **Description:** Concise description of issue/idea (e.g. “Data pipeline lag causes processing delays”).
  **Impact:** Category (Performance, Reliability, Security, etc.) and priority.
  **Action Plan:** Proposed fix or investigation (e.g. “Optimize Spark job parallelism”).
  **Owner:** Who will address it.
  **Metrics:** How success is measured (e.g. reduce lag from 500ms to <100ms).
  **Status:** To Do / In Progress / Done.
