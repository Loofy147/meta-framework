**System Engineering Plan (SEP) Template:**

- **Purpose:** Scope and objectives of the SEP.
- **Roles & Responsibilities:** Definition of key SE roles and their accountabilities.
- **Processes:** Tailored SE processes (from ISO 15288) to be used.
- **Schedule & Milestones:** Key technical reviews and decision gates.
- **Risk Management:** How risks will be identified, analyzed, and mitigated.
- **Configuration Management:** Strategy for versioning and change control of system artifacts.
- **Verification & Validation:** Plan for ensuring the system meets requirements and stakeholder needs.
