**Architecture Template:**
- **Overview:** System context and objectives.
- **Components:** List of modules/services with descriptions.
- **Data Flow:** Diagrams (e.g. SysML blocks, data pipeline sketch).
- **Interfaces:** Inputs/outputs for each component.
- **Decisions:** Key design decisions and alternatives.
- **Constraints:** Technology constraints (e.g. latency < 100ms, cloud-only deployment).
